Meet Vanessa Matlalcuatzi

Founder, homebaker, mama extraordinaire

Vanessa is a homebaker and small business owner who brings so much joy to her baking, the love comes through like a sugar rush in every bite. Food is her calling. After working in the food industry for nearly two decades, Vanessa finally decided to launch her own online bake shop, where she could share her creations and make treats that spread the love. Mommy's Sugar Rush was born in 2019.
